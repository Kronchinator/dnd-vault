# Bardic Inspiration
*Bard Class Feature*

**Action:** Bonus action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Duration:** 10 minutes

---

## Description

You can inspire others through stirring words or music. To do so, you use a **bonus action** on your turn to choose **one creature other than yourself** within 60 feet of you who can hear you. That creature gains one **Bardic Inspiration die**, a **d6**.

Once within the next 10 minutes, the creature can roll the die and **add the number rolled** to one:
- **Ability check**
- **Attack roll**
- **Saving throw**

The creature can wait until after it rolls the d20 before deciding to use the Bardic Inspiration die, but must decide before the DM says whether the roll succeeds or fails. Once used, the Bardic Inspiration die is gone.

A creature can only have one Bardic Inspiration die at a time.

## Notes
- Best spent on allies in high-stakes moments — saving throws vs. deadly effects, attack rolls on a crucial hit, skill checks that determine major outcomes.
- Do **not** waste it on routine ability checks with low stakes.
- At level 5, Aeritch gains **Font of Inspiration** — regain uses on short rest too.
- At level 5, Aeritch also gains a second subclass-dependent feature depending on her college.
