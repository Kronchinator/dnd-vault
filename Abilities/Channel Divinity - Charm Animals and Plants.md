# Channel Divinity: Charm Animals and Plants
*Nature Domain — Unlocks at Level 2*

**Action:** Action
**Range:** 30 feet (affects all eligible targets simultaneously)
**Duration:** 1 minute

---

## Description

As an action, you present your holy symbol and invoke the name of your deity. Each beast or plant creature that can see you within 30 feet of you must make a **Wisdom saving throw**. If the creature fails its saving throw, it is **charmed** by you for 1 minute or until it takes damage.

While charmed by you, the creature is friendly to you and your companions.

---

## Ressi's Stats

- **Spell Save DC:** 14 (WIS-based)
- **Uses per rest:** 1 (Channel Divinity recharges on a short or long rest)

---

## Charmed Condition

A charmed creature:
- Cannot attack the charmer or target them with harmful spells or effects
- The charmer has advantage on Charisma checks against the creature
- Charm ends immediately if the creature takes damage

---

## Notes

- Affects **all** beasts and plant creatures within 30 ft simultaneously — no limit on number of targets.
- No damage required — purely social/control. Charmed creatures become friendly, not compelled servants.
- Outstanding for crowd control in nature-heavy encounters (wolves, bears, animated plants, treants).
- The charm ends on damage — be careful about party members attacking charmed creatures accidentally.
- Combine with [[Animal Friendship]] and [[Speak with Animals]] for a full nature-diplomacy toolkit.
- Channel Divinity recharges on a **short or long rest** — use it freely, it comes back fast.
