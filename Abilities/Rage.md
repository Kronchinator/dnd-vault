# Rage
*Barbarian Class Feature*

**Action:** Bonus action
**Duration:** 1 minute (ends early if you end turn without attacking or taking damage since last turn, or if you are knocked unconscious)

---

## Description

On your turn, you can enter a rage as a bonus action. While raging, you gain the following benefits **if you aren't wearing heavy armour**:

- **Advantage** on Strength checks and Strength saving throws
- **+2 bonus** to damage rolls on melee weapon attacks using Strength
- **Resistance** to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage

Rage ends early if you are knocked unconscious, or if your turn ends and you haven't attacked a hostile creature since your last turn and haven't taken damage since then.

## Notes

- The +2 damage applies only to **melee attacks using STR**. Thrown javelins qualify.
- Rage damage resistance stacks with Iron Frame's AC — Melogg is extremely durable while raging.
- Raging while at range (bow, thrown) means you don't get the damage bonus but keep STR advantage and damage resistance.
- Rage does not prevent spellcasting, but Barbarians cannot cast spells while raging.
