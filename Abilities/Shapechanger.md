# Shapechanger
*Changeling Racial Trait*

---

## Description

As an **action**, you can polymorph your appearance to resemble any humanoid you have seen. You can change:

- Height and weight (within Small to Medium range)
- Skin tone, hair colour/texture/length
- Eye colour
- Facial structure and features
- Voice

You **cannot**:
- Duplicate a specific known individual exactly (you can create someone who looks *like* them, not *is* them)
- Change your equipment or clothing
- Change your size category (cannot become Small if Medium, or vice versa)
- Replicate non-humanoid features (wings, tails, etc.)

Your new appearance lasts until you use this feature again or die. You can revert to your true form at any time as part of the same action.
