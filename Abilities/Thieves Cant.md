# Thieves' Cant
*Rogue Class Feature*

---

## Description

During your rogue training you learned **Thieves' Cant** — a secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows you to hide messages in seemingly normal conversation.

It takes **four times longer** to convey a message in Thieves' Cant than it does to speak the same idea plainly.

You also understand a set of secret signs and symbols used by thieves' guilds to convey information — things like whether a location is dangerous, controlled by a guild, whether there's a safe house nearby, or whether locals are trustworthy.

---

## Notes

- Not a spell — purely social and informational utility.
- Other rogues, thieves' guild members, and those specifically trained in the cant can understand it.
- Useful for John to pass information to contacts or read guild signs in cities.
