# Campaign Plan — Act 1 Seeds (Sessions 2–4)

> ⚠️ **DM-ONLY.** A planting guide. The goal of Act 1 is to leave the party with a fistful of unexplained *wrongness* and zero answers. Read [[The Hollow Crown]] and [[The Seal of Thoria]] for what these seeds pay off into. **Golden rule: plant, never water.** If the party asks "what does it mean?", the honest in-world answer is *nobody here knows.*

## The three things you are secretly building toward
1. **The gold.** A signature the party will eventually associate with the true enemy. Every appearance is sensory and unexplained: golden light, amber/resin, the smell of preserved flowers.
2. **Made/unmade people.** A quiet drumbeat that some beings are *built* — Ressi's face, Melogg's curse, the faceless changelings, Amoris's literal nature.
3. **The land is being emptied.** The Greenward, the poverty, the tiredness in people — all symptoms of the same wound, never named as connected.

## Session 3 — The escape & the Greenward
*Spine: break out, deal with the fallout, and let the party investigate the Greenward on their own terms now that they have a reason to.*

**Seeds to plant:**
- **The Greenward, up close.** [[Maret]]'s sketch, the geometric spiral roots, the black grass in a **handprint too large to be human** ([[Pell]] drew it inside his boot). Ressi's DC 13 Nature/Religion: the roots spiral *downward, deliberately* — something beneath is pulling them. Amoris's brand aches and, if she presses her branded palm to the black grass, the ache becomes almost a *pull* — like the ground recognises the mark. **No explanation.**
- **The faceless changeling.** If the party stakes out the green at midnight, they meet (or chase) the figure [[Maret]] saw — a frightened person, outline blurring, with a **brand like Amoris's.** They flee or beg to be left alone. First proof Amoris is *not the only one marked.* (Ties [[The Seal of Thoria]].)
- **The Old Chord, in passing.** Route the party past [[The Old Chord]] shrine. As [[Aeritch Ravendeer|Aeritch]] passes with the [[Lyre of Veilsong]], the shrine **hums** — a single sympathetic note from the stone, gone when she stops. The central stone has three triangular indentations. Let them notice; don't let them solve it. The resin-smell from the cell is here too.
- **Goatius, closer.** A goat is now *definitely* there — on a wall, in an alley — and reacts to [[Melogg]] specifically. If a PC approaches, it ambles off with un-goatlike purpose. Melogg's stone is warm.

**Optional payoff for the escape:** the party can hit the [[The Weighing House]] on the way out — destroy ledgers, take back gear, find Aeritch's name flagged *"High priority. Do not approach directly. Notify Brek"* in a hand that is **not Corvin's.** First hard sign the orders come from above the Alberich.

## Session 4 — The road out, and the first thread of the larger web
*Spine: Aldenmire is too hot now; the party leaves (toward [[Ravencroft]], [[Meridh]], or wherever a hook pulls). Use travel to start braiding personal threads into the larger one — still without naming the Crown.*

**Seeds to plant (pick 2–3; don't dump all):**
- **Decoy BBEG breadcrumb.** Someone — a fleeing scholar, a dying cultist, a line in [[Damius Rooflane]]'s recovered notes — references a figure who "makes people," gilds what he touches, and is *very old.* Let the party start assembling a "dark sorcerer" they want to hunt. **This is the bait.** ([[The Hollow Crown|Gilded King]].)
- **John's artifact.** [[John Jackbox|John]] sees or hears something that snags on the thing the Alberich made him fetch — a matching sigil, a buyer asking after "the piece from the south job." He doesn't know what he recovered. Start making him wonder. (Ties [[The Seal of Thoria]].)
- **Ressi's geography problem.** Ressi tries to point someone toward The Painted Hollow on a map and the name **slides off** — an old NPC almost remembers it, then can't. [[Ressi Res Essiressires|Tigger]] goes still and hides when the name is spoken aloud. (Ties [[Ressi Res Essiressires]].)
- **Kallista, reaching.** The first real Kallista seed, kept deniable: a dream Amoris has of mushrooms and starlight and a girl's voice she can't quite hear; or the [[Kallista's Brooch]] (if Amoris carries it) is faintly **warm** and smells of resin — the same smell as Corvin's gold. The connection should be felt, not understood. ([[The Hollow Crown]].)
- **The Ashwing's shadow.** Word that the dragonborn is moving, asking after the elf — and a rumour, dropped once, that he's "looking for his wife." (Recommended payoff: [[Anakis]]. Don't confirm.)

## What NOT to reveal in Act 1 (hard list)
- That there is a seal, an imprisoned entity, or a Hollow Crown at all.
- That the gold is the same power as Amoris's brand / the Greenward / Kallista.
- That Kallista is alive / the vessel. (Treat her as purely dead backstory until Act 3.)
- That [[Ressi Res Essiressires|Ressi]] and [[Melogg]] were *made.* (Their own files govern those reveals; keep them personal-mystery, not lore-dump.)
- That the Architect is a victim, not a tyrant. (That's Reveal #1, Act 2→3.)

## Re-checking the work as you go
After each session, update [[Loose Threads]] and the relevant character/NPC files with what the party *actually* noticed. The reveals only land if you know exactly which seeds took root — track them so Act 3 can pay off the ones they remember and quietly re-seed the ones they missed.
