# Albertich Clan

## Overview
A family of ruthless mercantile ambition who believe the region's trade should answer to them alone. They are not simply criminals — they operate through documentation, legal grey areas, and strategic violence held just far enough at arm's length to avoid easy prosecution.

Eight months ago, they established a tax post on the Greyvein Bridge in [[Aldenmire]]. They have no legal authority there. Everyone pays anyway.

## Known Operations
- **[[Aldenmire]] tax post** — collectors on the Greyvein Bridge levy tolls on all merchant traffic entering or leaving town
- **[[The Weighing House]]** — Albertich administrative office in Aldenmire, run by [[Corvin Fels]]
- **A barn outside Aldenmire** — a secondary location where confiscated goods are held before being moved; known to [[Pell]]
- **Ravencroft region** — the Albertich clan now occupies the former [[Ravendeer Clan]] estate

## Methods
- Documentation and ledgers as instruments of control
- Debt leverage — taking deeds as collateral
- Physical enforcement through contractors (the "grey coats")
- Targeted violence against those who resist openly
- Surveillance — they have an informant inside Aldenmire feeding them information

## Relationship to Players
The party has already made enemies of Corvin Fels and Brek. Their descriptions and capabilities have been reported up the chain. The Albertich clan now considers them a threat to operations.

They also have [[Aeritch Ravendeer]] marked as high priority — her name is in Corvin's ledger with a note to notify Brek rather than approach directly.

## DM Notes
- The Albertich clan is the immediate Act 1 antagonist but not the campaign's final enemy — [[The Ashwing]] has an agenda beyond their interests
- The informant inside Aldenmire is a slow-burn reveal
- Disrupting the Weighing House (destroying ledgers, the lockbox) would significantly set back operations
