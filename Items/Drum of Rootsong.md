# Drum of Rootsong

*A small frame drum stretched with bark-leather from an ancient treant. The bark-leather is burned away.*

## Description
The third of the three Ravendeer family instruments. Mira Ravendeer's instrument. Each strike sent vibrations into the earth, allowing the player to commune with the land — sensing what moved across it, what was buried beneath it, and in moments of great need, calling the slow wrath of the earth itself.

The Drum was found shattered beside Mira in the ruins of the Ravendeer coastal estate. The bark-leather was burned away.

Aeritch gathered the pieces and kept them.

## Current State
Destroyed. The frame is shattered. The bark-leather that gave it its power is gone. What remains is fragments of wood and memory.

## Connection to the Seal
The Drum of Rootsong is one of the three components of [[The Seal of Thoria]]. The central stone of [[The Old Chord]] shrine has an indentation sized exactly for it.

Notably, the Drum was an instrument that communes with the earth — and the earth beneath Aldenmire is showing signs of a seal under strain.
