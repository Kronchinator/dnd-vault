# Flute of Stillwater

*Carved from the petrified bone of a river spirit. Now a blackened husk.*

## Description
The second of the three Ravendeer family instruments. Caelen Ravendeer's instrument. It was carved from the petrified bone of a river spirit and could manipulate the emotions of those who heard it — lulling rage into calm, coaxing despair into hope, or in darker hands, the reverse.

Caelen was known to use it to de-escalate tense trade negotiations before they turned to bloodshed.

The Flute was found destroyed in the ruins of the Ravendeer coastal estate. Caelen was still clutching the blackened husk when Aeritch found him.

She kept it.

## Current State
The real instrument is gone. Aeritch carries the blackened shell — it produces no sound, holds no magic. It is a relic of her brother and a reminder of what was taken.

## Connection to the Seal
The Flute of Stillwater is one of the three components of [[The Seal of Thoria]]. The central stone of [[The Old Chord]] shrine has an indentation sized exactly for it.

With the Flute destroyed, the seal cannot be completed as it once was — or perhaps another means exists.

## DM Notes
- The destroyed state of the Flute (and [[Drum of Rootsong]]) is a major obstacle for any seal-restoration arc
- Aeritch does not yet know the instruments' connection to the seal
- What happens if a destroyed component is brought to the shrine is unresolved — a puzzle for later acts
