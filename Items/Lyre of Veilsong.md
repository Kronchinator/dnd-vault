# Lyre of Veilsong

*A slender lyre strung with threads spun from moonspider silk. It survived dragonfire unscathed.*

## Description
The first of the three Ravendeer family instruments. Its frame is pale wood, almost white, with tuning pegs carved in a spiral curl that matches exactly the instrument depicted in the carvings of [[The Old Chord]] shrine.

When played, it can weave illusions into the minds of listeners — making them perceive what the player wills. An empty room full of grandeur. A stranger wearing a familiar face. Memories half-remembered that never truly occurred.

Aeritch plays it when she needs to think, or to remember.

## Why It Survived
The Lyre was found unscathed in the ruins of the Ravendeer coastal estate. The dragonfire stepped around it. Nobody has explained this. It may not have been chance.

## Mechanical Effect
*Full mechanics to be established when Aeritch attunes. Current known properties:*
- Hums audibly when within 30 feet of [[The Old Chord]] shrine — the hum matches no note in any known scale
- When pointed toward the Greenward's spiral centre, hums almost inaudibly (Aeritch only)
- Can create illusions when played — scope and limits to be determined with player

## Connection to the Seal
The three Ravendeer instruments — the Lyre, [[Flute of Stillwater]], and [[Drum of Rootsong]] — are the three components of [[The Seal of Thoria]]. The central stone of the Old Chord shrine has three indentations in a triangular arrangement, sized exactly for each instrument.

The Lyre is the only one of the three that survived intact.
