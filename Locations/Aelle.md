# Aelle

## Description
Aelle is the kingdom Prince Csesper comes from. Its court is separate from [[Ravencroft]], and Csesper's proposed marriage to [[Princess Kallista]] was a political alliance between kingdoms — not Ravencroft succession.

For now, keep Aelle lightly defined: enough to anchor Csesper's origin, but loose enough to shape when the campaign needs it.

## Key Figures
| Person | Role |
|--------|------|

## Current Status
Aelle's current politics are unresolved. What matters now is the correction: Csesper is not Ravencroft royalty. He was a foreign prince present at Ravencroft because of the intended marriage alliance.

## DM Notes
- Decide later whether Aelle is an ally, rival, or nervous diplomatic partner to Ravencroft.
- Csesper's decision to help [[Amoris Scilia|Amoris]] flee may have strained relations between Aelle and Ravencroft.
- Aelle can become a future route for Csesper's reintroduction if the party leaves Aldenmire/Ravencroft politics behind.
