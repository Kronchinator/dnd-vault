# Aldenmire

## Description
A crossroads town where the Greyvein river meets the north merchant road. Aldenmire is the kind of place that used to be something — solid stone foundations, a working fountain in the market square, children in the streets. It smells like sawdust, river mud, and woodsmoke. The buildings are stone at the base, timber on top, built to last but patched too many times.

Eight months ago, the Alberich clan established a tax post on the Greyvein bridge. They have no legal authority. Everyone pays anyway.

The people of Aldenmire walk with their heads slightly down. Conversations stop when strangers pass.

## Districts & Key Locations

## Atmosphere Notes
- Daytime: quiet industry, people working with heads down
- Evening: most locals go home early; the common room of the Tillerman's Rest is never loud
- Night: the streets are empty; the Greenward is dark and soundless
