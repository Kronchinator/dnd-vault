# Meridh

## Description
A modest port town far enough from the city that no one asks questions. The kind of place where people arrive with less than they're willing to say and leave before anyone starts wondering. The docks smell like salt and rope. The taverns stay open late.

[[Aeritch Ravendeer]] spent years here after her family's death, long enough that it shaped who she became.

## Key Locations
- **The Salted Crow** — the tavern where Aeritch busked, night after night, until she had what she needed
- **[[Yujie]]'s establishment** — underground, discreet, dangerous, and by its own code principled. Not on any map.

## Key Figures
| Person | Role |
|--------|------|
| [[Yujie]] | Ran the underground establishment; gave Aeritch her footing |
| [[Damius Rooflane]] | Elderly wizard; Aeritch's mentor; died here |

## What Happened Here
Aeritch arrived with dwindling coin and grief she hadn't processed. She began busking at the Salted Crow. Yujie found her in a bad situation and made an offer. She took it.

Over years she built a network of favours — men of influence who thought they were indulging, not being catalogued. She learned people the way some people learn languages: thoroughly, with attention to what isn't said.

Then she met Damius Rooflane, who taught her spells the way she'd always wanted to be taught — because she was curious, not because she was useful to him. He died and left her everything.

She left Meridh prepared for what comes next.
