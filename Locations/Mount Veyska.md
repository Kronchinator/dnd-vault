# Mount Veyska

## Overview

Mount Veyska is the mountain homeland of [[Goatius Everest]].

To most people, it is just a remote mountain somewhere beyond the practical concerns of civilized maps. To Goatius, it is home: high wind, hard stone, sharp ledges, and the finest goats the world has ever had the wisdom to produce.

Goatius wants to return there. Badly.

---

## Known Details

- Goatius was taken from Mount Veyska before being sacrificed by cultists.
- His deal with [[Matthew]] includes being sent or guided back home.
- The mountain may be real, mythologized, magically hidden, or simply very far away.
- It can function as a future comic destination, emotional destination, or both.

---

## DM Notes

Mount Veyska should be treated as funny on the surface but emotionally real underneath. Goatius's desire is crude and ridiculous, but it is still homesickness. The cleaner version: he wants his old life back and is too much of a goat to say that elegantly.

Potential uses:

- A later travel destination.
- A place where Goatius's true nature becomes obvious.
- A location connected to old demonic roads, because Matthew's bargain may have marked it.
- A surprisingly dangerous mountain full of goats that are much smarter than anyone wants.
