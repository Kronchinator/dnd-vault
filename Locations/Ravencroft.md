# Ravencroft

## Description
A kingdom close enough to Aldenmire that [[Amoris Scilia|Amoris]] is uneasy being here. It has a king, a royal court, a history of Astrologers who map the stars and bridge constellations to the earth — and one missing princess who died in the woods a year ago.

Most people in Aldenmire have no reason to think about Ravencroft. Amoris thinks about nothing else.

## Current Status
Ravencroft considers Amoris a fugitive, wanted for the murder of Princess Kallista. The King believes Amoris killed her — or at minimum that she is responsible for the princess's disappearance. The truth has not reached anyone who could act on it.

Kallista's body is still hidden in the woods outside the castle. If found, evidence could exonerate Amoris — or further incriminate her, depending on the state of the remains and who finds them.

## The Astrologer Tradition
Ravencroft has a tradition of Astrologers who map the stars and bridge constellations to the earth. Kallista was destined to be named Astrologer on her 21st birthday — the same night the King uncovered Amoris's deception.

Whether the Astrologer tradition connects to the [[The Seal of Thoria|Seal]] or the imprisoned entity beneath Thoria is unresolved.

## DM Notes
- Ravencroft is Amoris's primary motivation — she needs to clear her name and avenge Kallista
- The kingdom's proximity to Aldenmire is a constant background pressure for Amoris
- Prince Csesper is a potential ally if reintroduced; his motives for helping Amoris are unresolved, and his Aellean origin may matter diplomatically
