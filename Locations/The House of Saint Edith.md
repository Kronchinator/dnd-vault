# The House of Saint Edith

## Overview

The House of Saint Edith is the orphanage where [[Melogg]] believes he was raised.

In Melogg's memory, he was abandoned there as an infant and grew up surrounded by children he came to see as brothers and sisters. It was not a wealthy place, but it was home.

After Melogg was transformed into a golem, he returned there seeking comfort. Instead, the caretakers and children panicked, believed him to be a monster, and drove him away.

---

## DM Secret

The House may be real, false, or altered.

Keep its truth flexible until needed. If it is real, it can become a painful location for Melogg to revisit. If it is false, it becomes evidence that his identity was built from implanted memories. If it is partially real, it may reveal that someone else's life was used to construct Melogg's story.

---

## Possible Reveal Details

- Records contain no Melogg, but do contain similar names.
- The orphanage burned down years before Melogg remembers living there.
- The current caretakers recognize the story of the cursed child as local folklore.
- A child from Melogg's memory is now an old adult, proving time moved strangely or the memory belongs to someone else.
