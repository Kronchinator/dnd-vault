# The Weighing House

## Description
The old merchant guild hall — a solid two-storey building near the market square, repurposed as the Albertich clan's local administrative office eight months ago. The original guild sign has been taken down; the doorframe now bears a small brass plate engraved with a set of weighing scales.

Inside it is fastidiously clean. Everything has a place. There are two desks, a lockbox bolted to the floor, shelves of ledgers sorted by month, and a second room in the back that nobody outside has been in.

It smells like ink, beeswax, and something faintly metallic.

## Notable Features
- **The Front Desk** — Where Corvin Fels receives people. A ledger is always open. A chair sits across from the desk for whoever is being documented.
- **The Lockbox** — Floor-bolted, iron, combination lock. Contains collected coin.
- **The Ledgers** — Every transaction, every name, every debt recorded in Corvin's precise hand. A goldmine of information if the party can access them.
- **The Back Room** — Corvin's private room. Locked. Contains correspondence with the Albertich clan hierarchy, including letters referencing the Ashwing's movements.
