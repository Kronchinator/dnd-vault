# Barkimedes

## Description

Barkimedes is [[Oswin Dray|Oswin]]'s dog at [[The Tillerman's Rest]]. Big, loyal, not especially tactical, and possessed of the sort of heroic stupidity that makes animals better than people and worse at surviving villains.

He usually stays under the bar, watching doors and tracking tension before anyone else admits it is there.

---

## Session 02 Role

In [[Session 02 — Standard Procedure]], Barkimedes is the emotional break in [[Corvin Fels|Corvin]]'s control scene.

When Corvin's gilded magic has the party restrained, Barkimedes attacks him. The attack briefly disrupts Corvin's concentration and gives the party one doomed moment of agency before the gold reasserts itself.

This beat exists to:

- Show that Corvin's power can be disrupted.
- Give the party one meaningful choice inside the scripted capture.
- Make the capture personal instead of merely tactical.
- Make the party hate Corvin. Efficient villain work. Unsubtle, but effective.

---

## DM Notes

- Use carefully. This is animal cruelty as a dramatic tool; do the table-safety check before running it.
- Barkimedes should feel loyal, frightened, and brave rather than comic in the moment.
- If the table reacts badly, soften the consequence: injured, taken, or magically stunned instead of killed.
- If the table leans in, this becomes a clean emotional wound tied to Oswin, Aldenmire, and Corvin.
