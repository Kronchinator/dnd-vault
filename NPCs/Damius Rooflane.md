# Damius Rooflane

## Description
An old man of considerable magical power and considerably more loneliness. He had outlived his entire family and carried his accumulated knowledge the way some men carry regret — heavily, and with nowhere to put it down.

He found in Aeritch something he had given up hoping to find: a student who asked about his life, not just his spells.

He died peacefully in his study, surrounded by books. He left everything to her.

## Personality
- Generous with knowledge once trust was established
- Kept his grief close and his gratitude closer
- Did not perform wisdom; he simply had it

## Secrets
- [[The Promissory Ring]] has Yujie's name on it, crossed out — Damius and Yujie knew each other. The nature of that relationship is unrevealed.
- Another name in the ring, not crossed out, belongs to someone still alive and still owed something. Aeritch does not know who yet.
- He knew more about the Ravendeer instruments than he ever said — how much, and why, is a thread.

## Relationship to Players
Aeritch genuinely cared for him. He taught her the architecture of spells and left her his spellbooks and the Promissory Ring. He is gone, but his debts and his secrets travel with her.
