# Goatius Everest

## Description
Unknown. The party has not seen him. He is always nearby — watching from across a street, sitting in a corner of a room, standing in a crowd. He does not draw attention. Whether he wants to be found is unclear.

The rune on the underside of [[Goatius's Stone]] is his mark.

## Personality
*To be revealed.*

## Goals
- Keep Melogg within the 100-metre tether range

## Secrets
- **The Tether** — Melogg is mystically bound to Goatius at a range of 100 metres. If Melogg moves beyond this distance, Melogg cannot move or speak. The party does not know this.
- Goatius gave Melogg the stone deliberately; it is a tracking anchor that keeps the tether stable
- The warmth of the stone increases when Goatius is within 30 feet
- The rune on the stone's underside is his personal mark — very old script, unidentified even on an Arcana DC 18 success

## Relationship to Players
The party has not met him. He has been watching them since before they arrived. Session 2 is when he should be *almost* noticed — a glimpse from across the street, a figure that isn't there when they look again.
