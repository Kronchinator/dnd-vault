# Lady Timbra

## Description
A woman of some local standing in Aldenmire — not nobility, but the kind of person who dresses like she is and expects to be treated accordingly. She wears her identity in the details: a particular necklace she never takes off, a particular way of carrying herself in the market.

[[Amoris Scilia]] copied her appearance using changeling abilities, presumably after observing her in town.

## The Necklace
Lady Timbra owns a necklace — a regular piece of jewellery she wears as part of her daily appearance. When Amoris copied her form, she copied everything, including the necklace. The copied necklace is not a real object — it is part of Amoris's skin, a visual element of the changeling form. It cannot be removed.

[[John Jackbox]] attempted to steal it in Session 1, reaching for it while Amoris was still in Lady Timbra's form. The necklace did not come free because there was nothing to take — it had no physical existence separate from Amoris's body. John's hand found only skin.
