# Princess Kallista

## Description
One year older than Amoris, slight and bright-eyed, with a mind that latched onto things and didn't let go. She had a running list of hyperfixations — mushrooms, animals, nature, philosophy, literature, science — and she pursued each one with the total investment of someone who had never learned to do things halfway.

She was kind in the way that comes from actually paying attention to people.

She wore a small brooch every day: a pale mushroom bloom preserved in resin, sealed there by a castle jeweller as a joke gift. She didn't take jokes lightly.

She died touching a cursed rune in the woods outside Ravencroft. She was twenty years old.

## Personality
- Curious to the point of recklessness
- Utterly loyal to Amoris once she knew the truth about her nature
- Found Amoris's shapeshifting wonderful rather than frightening
- Would have been an extraordinary Astrologer

## Secrets
- [[Kallista's Brooch]] absorbed a trace of the cursed rune at the moment of her death — the brooch is the only physical object that carries that moment
- Her body is still hidden where Amoris left it, in the woods outside Ravencroft
- The rune was not placed there randomly — the trunk was a trap, and the intended target is unclear

## Relationship to Players
Amoris loved her more than anyone. She is the reason Amoris is wandering, the reason the rune brand exists, and the reason Amoris cannot go home.

The party does not know about her yet.
