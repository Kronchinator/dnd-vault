# Yujie

## Description
A woman who moves like a closed fist — sharp-dressed, purposeful, surrounded by people who follow her without being told to. She runs an underground establishment in Meridh that is discreet, dangerous, and surprisingly principled by its own code.

She pulled Aeritch out of a bad situation at the Salted Crow without being asked. Then she asked a few questions. Then she made an offer.

## Personality
- Assesses everything quickly and accurately
- Does not romanticise what she does; this is a business with its own ethics
- Thorough — she made sure Aeritch was of legal age before the conversation went anywhere
- Respects competence and self-possession in others

## Goals
- Run her establishment; maintain its reputation for discretion
- Unknown longer-term agenda

## Secrets
- Her name appears in [[The Promissory Ring]], crossed out — she settled a debt with Damius Rooflane at some point. What she owed him, or he owed her, is unknown.
- She may know more about the Ravendeer family than she has ever said — her path crossed Damius Rooflane's, and Damius was not ignorant of things

## Relationship to Players
Aeritch considers her a complicated ally or a complication — it's not clear which. The party does not know she exists.
