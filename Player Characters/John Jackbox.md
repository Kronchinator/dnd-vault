## Backstory

John Jackbox is a half-Malay, half-French thief from the poor streets of [[Marsh-elling]]. Since he was young, he always saw rich people living easy lives while normal people suffered every day. His family could barely earn enough money because of unfair socioeconomic circumstances, corrupt nobles, and high taxes. Seeing this made him slowly hate the rich and dream about starting a revolution against the upper class one day.

At first, it started small: stealing food, coins, and robbing drunk nobles in dark alleyways just to survive. But over time, he became more skilled and more greedy too. He slowly moved to stealing gold, expensive valuables, and gambling in underground taverns and criminal hideouts. Around the streets, people started hearing the name “Jackbox” because nobody could ever catch him for long, and he always had some smart way to escape.

One day, the [[Alberich Clan]] hired him for a big job. They manipulated him by saying they were on his side and also hated corruption in the kingdom. They promised him enough money to help his family and maybe finally change the poor districts of [[Marsh-elling]]. But little did he know, the [[Alberich Clan]] themselves were part of the rich upper class keeping everyone poor while they became richer from war, taxes, and suffering.

They sent him on a dangerous, life-threatening journey across ruined lands, monster-infested forests, and bandit territory to recover a valuable artifact. Its exact place in the deeper lore is still unknown to Jackbox, but he may discover the truth during the campaign. After he finally came back barely alive, the [[Alberich Clan]] scammed and betrayed him, taking all the money and blaming crimes on him so nobody would suspect them. Soon bounty hunters, mercenaries, and guards across the kingdom were hunting him down.

Since then, Jackbox became far more dangerous, always moving from town to town and never staying in one place too long. He works alone now because after what happened, he barely trusts anybody anymore. Over the years, he became colder, smarter, and more ruthless, surviving through manipulation, stealth, gambling, and theft. Now he steals from rich people not just to survive, but also to slowly build power and money for the day he finally gets revenge on the [[Alberich Clan]] and destroys everything they built.
