## Backstory

Kael was raised in a small faith-bound order known as **The Ashbound Canticle**: a group devoted not to conquest, but to remembrance, endurance, and the belief that redemption can still exist even after ruin.

As an Aasimar, Kael was set apart from other people from a young age. The Order raised him to believe that divinity was not superiority, but responsibility. If he had light in him, then it was not a crown. It was a burden to carry carefully.

During a period of conflict, Kael was tasked with protecting civilians caught between warring forces. The Ashbound Canticle was eventually overwhelmed, and Kael became one of the few survivors — not because he was stronger than the others, but because he refused to abandon the wounded.

After the conflict subsided, Kael took up a mission alone: travelling from settlement to settlement, offering protection and healing while learning more about the wider world. He eventually embraced the path of a Paladin, swearing the **Oath of Devotion** not as blind obedience, but as a conscious commitment to protect innocence and resist corruption wherever it appears.
