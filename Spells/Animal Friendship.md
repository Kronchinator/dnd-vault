# Animal Friendship
*1st-level Enchantment*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)
**Duration:** 24 hours

---

## Description

This spell lets you convince a beast that you mean it no harm. Choose a beast that you can see within range. It must see and hear you. If the beast's Intelligence is 4 or higher, the spell fails.

The beast must succeed on a **Wisdom saving throw** or be **charmed** by you for the spell's duration. If you or one of your companions harms the target, the spell ends.

| Slot Level | Beasts Affected |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 1st | 1 beast |
| 2nd | 2 beasts |
| 3rd | 3 beasts |
| *(+1 per slot above 1st)* | |

---

## Ressi's Stats

- **Spell Save DC:** 14
- **Always prepared** (Nature Domain spell — does not count against prepared spell limit)

---

## Notes

- Only works on beasts with **Intelligence 4 or lower** — won't work on most monsters, humanoids, or intelligent creatures.
- Charmed beast won't attack Ressi, but doesn't make it an ally or give commands over it.
- Pairs well with [[Speak with Animals]] — charm the beast first, then have a conversation.
- Useful for bypassing guard animals, calming wildlife, or making a temporary animal companion.
- The morsel of food is a minor material component — easy to have on hand.
