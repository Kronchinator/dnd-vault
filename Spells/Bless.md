# Bless
*1st-level Enchantment*

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** 30 ft  
**Components:** V, S, M  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

## Description

Choose up to **three creatures** within range. Whenever a target makes an attack roll or saving throw before the spell ends, the target adds **1d4** to the roll.

At higher levels, target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

---

## Kael's Notes

- Prepared as a Paladin spell and also chosen through **Magic Initiate (Cleric)** for 1/long rest without spending a spell slot.
- Excellent first-round concentration spell when the party expects a fight.
- Competes with [[Shield of Faith]] and [[Protection from Evil and Good]] for concentration.
