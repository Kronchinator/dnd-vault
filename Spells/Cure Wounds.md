# Cure Wounds

**1st-level Evocation**
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

## Effect
A creature you touch regains hit points equal to **1d8 + your spellcasting modifier** (Ressi: 1d8 + 4).

Has no effect on undead or constructs.

## At Higher Levels
When cast using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

## Key Rules
- **Action** — Costs a full Action, unlike Healing Word. Cannot heal and also cast a cantrip in the same turn.
- **Touch range** — Ressi must be adjacent to the target (within 5 ft)
- **No effect on constructs** — Cannot be used on Melogg (he is a construct)
- Use [[Healing Word]] when you need to stay back or still act. Use Cure Wounds when you can afford the action and want higher healing.

## Ressi's Healing Output
| Slot Level | Healing |
|---|---|
| 1st | 1d8 + 4 (avg 8) |
| 2nd | 2d8 + 4 (avg 13) |
| 3rd | 3d8 + 4 (avg 17) |

## Healing Word vs Cure Wounds
| | Healing Word | Cure Wounds |
|---|---|---|
| Action Cost | Bonus action | Full action |
| Range | 60 ft | Touch |
| Healing (1st) | 1d4+4 (avg 6) | 1d8+4 (avg 8) |
| Best used when | Need to also act | Can afford full action |

## DM Note
Cure Wounds cannot heal Melogg — constructs are immune. This is worth flagging to Ressi's player early so they don't waste a slot mid-combat.
