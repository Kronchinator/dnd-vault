# Divine Smite
*1st-level Evocation — Paladin Smite*

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
**Range:** Self  
**Components:** V  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

After hitting a creature with a melee weapon or unarmed strike, empower the strike with divine force. The target takes extra **radiant damage**.

Kael always has this prepared from **Paladin's Smite** and can cast it **once per long rest without expending a spell slot**. He can also cast it using available spell slots.

---

## Kael's Notes

- Use after confirming a hit, ideally on an important target.
- Fits Kael's ember-halo and ash-scar imagery cleanly: not pristine holy light, more like burning mercy with teeth.
