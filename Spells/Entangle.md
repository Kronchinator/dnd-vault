# Entangle
*1st-level Conjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 90 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

## Description

Grasping weeds and vines sprout from the ground in a **20-foot square** originating from a point within range. For the duration, these plants turn the ground in the area into **difficult terrain**.

A creature in the area when you cast the spell must succeed on a **Strength saving throw** or be **restrained** by the entangling plants until the spell ends. A creature restrained by the plants can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, freeing itself on success.

When the spell ends, the conjured plants wilt away.

---

## Amoris's Stats

- **Spell Save DC:** 12 (8 + 2 prof + 2 WIS)

---

## Notes

- One of the strongest level 1 control spells. No damage, but **Restrained** is a powerful condition:
  - Restrained creatures have 0 speed
  - Attack rolls against them have advantage
  - Their own attack rolls have disadvantage
  - They have disadvantage on DEX saving throws
- The difficult terrain persists even after creatures escape — the area remains hazardous.
- Does **not** affect creatures that are flying or who have freedom of movement.
- Requires concentration — conflicts with Guidance.
- 20-foot square at 90 feet range is exceptional battlefield control.
