# Guidance
*Divination cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

---

## Description

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a **d4** and add the number rolled to **one ability check** of its choice.

The creature can roll the die before or after making the ability check. The spell then ends.

---

## Notes

- One of the most efficient cantrips in the game — essentially a free +1 to +4 on any ability check.
- Requires concentration, so cannot be active alongside Entangle or other concentration spells.
- Best used out of combat: picking locks, persuasion checks, investigation, athletics.
- Since it requires touch, Amoris must be adjacent to the target.
- Can be cast on herself.
