# Healing Word

**1st-level Evocation**
**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
**Range:** 60 ft
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Instantaneous

## Effect
A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to **1d4 + your spellcasting modifier** (Ressi: 1d4 + 4).

## At Higher Levels
When cast using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.

## Key Rules
- **Bonus Action** — This is the most important thing. Healing Word costs a bonus action, meaning Ressi can heal *and* still take a full Action on the same turn (attack, cast a cantrip, etc.)
- The target must be alive (above 0 HP) — use [[Spare the Dying]] for stabilising downed creatures instead
- Works at range (60 ft) — Ressi does not need to be adjacent to the target

## Ressi's Healing Output
| Slot Level | Healing |
|---|---|
| 1st | 1d4 + 4 (avg 6) |
| 2nd | 2d4 + 4 (avg 9) |
| 3rd | 3d4 + 4 (avg 12) |

## DM Tutorial Note
First time Ressi uses this, pause and explain: *"Healing Word is a Bonus Action. That means you can heal someone AND still cast Sacred Flame or make an attack on the same turn. Two things happen. Use it."*
