# Identify
*1st-level Divination (ritual)*

**Casting Time:** 1 minute (or 11 minutes as a ritual — no spell slot used)
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S, M (a pearl worth at least 100 gp and an owl feather)
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

You choose one object that you must touch throughout the casting of the spell. If it is a **magic item or other magic-imbued object**, you learn its properties and how to use them, whether it requires attunement, and how many charges it has if any. You learn whether any spells are affecting the item and what they are.

If the object you touch is a **creature**, you learn what spells, if any, are currently affecting it.

---

## Notes
- Cast as a **ritual** to avoid spending a spell slot (takes 11 minutes total).
- **Material component:** 100 gp pearl — this is consumed. Aeritch needs to track this.
- Essential for identifying unknown magic items found in the field.
- Does **not** reveal curses on items — that requires *Remove Curse* or attunement.
