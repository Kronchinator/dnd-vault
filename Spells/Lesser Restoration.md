# Lesser Restoration
*2nd-level Abjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

Touch one creature and end one condition affecting it:

- Blinded
- Deafened
- Paralyzed
- Poisoned

---

## Kael's Notes

- From **Celestial Legacy** at level 3: 1/long rest.
- Does not require concentration.
- Useful as emergency condition removal rather than raw healing.
