# Prestidigitation
*Transmutation cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 10 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Up to 1 hour

---

## Description

A minor magical trick that novice spellcasters use for practice. You create one of the following effects within range:

- **Sensory effect** — Create an instantaneous, harmless sensory effect such as a shower of sparks, a puff of wind, faint musical notes, or an odd odor.
- **Light/extinguish** — Instantaneously light or snuff out a candle, torch, or small campfire.
- **Clean/soil** — Instantaneously clean or soil an object no larger than 1 cubic foot.
- **Chill/warm/flavor** — Chill, warm, or flavor up to 1 cubic foot of nonliving material for 1 hour.
- **Color/mark** — Make a color, small mark, or symbol appear on an object or surface for 1 hour.
- **Trinket/image** — Create a nonmagical trinket or an illusory image that can fit in your hand and lasts until the end of your next turn.

You can have up to three of these effects active simultaneously. Dispelling an effect requires another action.

---

## Notes
- No combat application, but incredibly versatile for roleplay and utility.
- Great for Aeritch to use during performances — visual flourishes, scent, ambient music.
