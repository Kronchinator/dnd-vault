# Protection from Evil and Good
*1st-level Abjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

---

## Description

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against certain creature types: Aberrations, Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, and Undead.

Those creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls against the target. The target also cannot be charmed, frightened, or possessed by them. If already affected, the target has advantage on any new saving throw against the relevant effect.

---

## Kael's Notes

- Always prepared from **Oath of Devotion**.
- Very strong when facing undead, fiends, or other supernatural threats.
- Concentration spell; competes with [[Bless]] and [[Shield of Faith]].
