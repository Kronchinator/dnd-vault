# Sacred Flame
*Evocation cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 60 feet
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

Flame-like radiance descends on a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a **Dexterity saving throw** or take **1d8 radiant damage**.

The target gains **no benefit from cover** for this saving throw.

| Caster Level | Damage |
|-------------|--------|
| 1–4 | 1d8 radiant |
| 5–10 | 2d8 radiant |
| 11–16 | 3d8 radiant |
| 17+ | 4d8 radiant |

---

## Ressi's Stats

- **Spell Save DC:** 14 (8 + 2 prof + 4 WIS)
- **Current damage:** 1d8 radiant

---

## Notes

- Targets **DEX save**, not an attack roll — bypasses AC entirely.
- Cover is ignored — works around walls, shields, and partial cover.
- Radiant damage is rarely resisted (undead and fiends are sometimes vulnerable to it).
- Ressi's high WIS (DC 14) makes this a punishing cantrip even at level 1.
- The cleric's go-to combat cantrip.
