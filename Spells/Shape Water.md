# Shape Water
*Transmutation cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** S
**Duration:** Instantaneous or up to 1 hour

---

## Description

You choose an area of water that fits within a **5-foot cube** within range. You manipulate it in one of the following ways:

- **Move/reshape** — Instantly move or rearrange the water any way you choose within the area. The water doesn't flow beyond the area.
- **Animate** — Cause the water to form shapes and animate at your direction for up to 1 hour. (Ice shapes last until melted.)
- **Freeze** — Freeze the water, provided there are no creatures in it. The ice lasts for up to 1 hour or until broken.
- **Change colour/opacity** — Change the colour or opacity of the water for up to 1 hour.

If you cast this spell multiple times, you can have no more than **two** of its non-instantaneous effects active at a time.

---

## Notes

- Useful for revealing invisible creatures (coat in water), improvised tools (ice bridges, plugs, barriers), and signalling.
- Cannot be used as a weapon directly, but creative use can create hazards.
- Pairs well with Amoris's Druid nature theme.
