# Shield of Faith
*1st-level Abjuration*

**Casting Time:** 1 bonus action  
**Range:** 60 ft  
**Components:** V, S, M  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

---

## Description

A shimmering field appears around a creature of your choice within range, granting a **+2 bonus to AC** for the duration.

---

## Kael's Notes

- Always prepared from **Oath of Devotion** and also listed as a Paladin spell.
- With Kael's normal AC 19, casting this on himself raises him to **AC 21** while concentration holds.
- Often better used on whichever ally is about to be focused down.
