# Sleep
*1st-level Enchantment*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 90 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a pinch of fine sand, rose petals, or a cricket)
**Duration:** 1 minute

---

## Description

This spell sends creatures into a magical slumber. Roll **5d8** — the total is the number of hit points of creatures this spell can affect.

Starting with the creature that has the **lowest current HP** and working upward, each creature affected by this spell falls unconscious until the spell ends, the sleeper takes damage, or someone uses an action to shake or slap the sleeper awake.

- **Undead and creatures immune to charm are unaffected.**
- Creatures are affected in order from lowest to highest HP until the pool is exhausted.

| Slot Level | Dice |
|-----------|------|
| 1st | 5d8 |
| 2nd | 7d8 |
| 3rd | 9d8 |
| 4th | 11d8 |
| 5th | 13d8 |

*(+2d8 per slot level above 1st)*

---

## Notes
- **No saving throw** — one of the few control spells that bypasses saves entirely.
- Effectiveness depends entirely on enemy HP. Works great on minions and weakened enemies; useless on bosses.
- Average roll at 1st level: ~22 HP affected.
- Unconscious creatures can be **coup de graced** (auto-crit on melee, instant kill if damage ≥ twice max HP).
- Highly effective for non-lethal takedowns or capturing enemies alive.
- Does **not** affect elves (full elves) — they are immune to magical sleep. Aeritch as a High Elf is also immune.
