# Spare the Dying
*Necromancy cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Touch
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

You touch a living creature that has **0 hit points**. The creature becomes **stable** (stops making death saving throws).

This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

---

## Notes

- Does **not** restore HP — it only stabilises. A stabilised creature remains unconscious at 0 HP.
- Saves a spell slot compared to using Cure Wounds (which would also restore HP but costs a 1st-level slot).
- Use this when you just need someone to *not die*. Use a healing spell if you need them back in the fight.
- The requirement to touch means Ressi must move adjacent to the downed ally — risky in active combat.
- Invaluable at level 1 when allies are fragile and death saves are a real threat.
