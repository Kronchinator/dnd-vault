# Speak with Animals
*1st-level Divination (ritual)*

**Casting Time:** 1 action (ritual: 11 minutes)
**Range:** Self
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** 10 minutes

---

## Description

You gain the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with beasts for the duration. The knowledge and awareness of many beasts is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum, beasts can give you information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day.

You might be able to persuade a beast to perform a small favour for you, at the GM's discretion.

---

## Notes

- Cast as a **ritual** to avoid spending a spell slot (takes 11 minutes total — not usable in emergencies).
- Beasts have limited intelligence — don't expect nuanced testimony. Useful for: "Has anyone passed this road?", "What lives in those caves?", "Is the forest safe?"
- Persuading animals to help requires roleplay and DM discretion — not a guaranteed effect.
- Pairs perfectly with Ressi's thematic character kit and suggested Nature Domain.
- If Ressi has Nature Domain, this is always prepared (domain spell) — no slot needed.
