# Thaumaturgy
*Transmutation cantrip*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 30 feet
**Components:** V
**Duration:** Up to 1 minute

---

## Description

You manifest a minor wonder — a sign of supernatural power — within range. You create one of the following magical effects:

- **Voice boom** — Your voice booms up to 3 times as loud as normal for 1 minute
- **Flame flicker** — Cause flames to flicker, brighten, dim, or change colour for 1 minute
- **Tremor** — Cause harmless tremors in the ground for 1 minute
- **Sound effect** — Create an instantaneous sound originating from a point of your choice (thunder, a raven's caw, ominous whispers, etc.)
- **Unlocked doors** — Instantaneously cause an unlocked door or window to fly open or slam shut
- **Eye change** — Cause your eyes to change colour for 1 minute

You can have up to three of these effects active at once. Any effect you create ends after 1 minute.

---

## Notes

- Pure roleplay/utility cantrip. No combat value.
- Outstanding for intimidation, performance, and atmosphere.
