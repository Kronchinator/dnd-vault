# Thunderwave
*1st-level Evocation*

**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** Self (15-foot cube emanating from you)
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Instantaneous

---

## Description

A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you. Each creature in a **15-foot cube** originating from you must make a **Constitution saving throw**.

- **Failed save:** 2d8 thunder damage + pushed **10 feet** away from you.
- **Successful save:** Half damage, not pushed.

In addition, **unsecured objects** that are completely within the area of effect are automatically pushed 10 feet away from you by the spell's effect, and the spell emits a **thunderous boom** audible out to 300 feet.

| Slot Level | Damage |
|-----------|--------|
| 1st | 2d8 |
| 2nd | 3d8 |
| 3rd | 4d8 |
| 4th | 5d8 |
| 5th | 6d8 |

*(+1d8 per slot level above 1st)*

**Spell Save DC for Aeritch:** 13 (8 + 2 prof + 3 CHA)

---

## Notes
- Excellent for pushing enemies off ledges, out of grapples, or away from downed allies.
- The loud boom **alerts everyone within 300 feet** — avoid using in stealth scenarios.
- Targets creatures in a cube in front of you, not a full 360° — positioning matters.
- No concentration, no saving throw to avoid damage entirely (half on success).
- Best used when clustered enemies are near a hazard (pit, cliff, fire).
